I met him first, I say, in 1929 through the intervention of a friend of my common friend who asked Mr. Gandhi to see me, you see. So Mr. Gandhi wrote to me saying that he would like to see me. So I went and saw him. That was just before going to the round table conference. And then he came to the second round table conference. Didn't come for the first. And he was there for about 5-6 months. There of course I met him also and faced him also. You see. Then at Once thereafter, he also after the signing of the Poona Pact, you see, asked me to come and see him. So I went to see him. He was then in jail. You see, that's all the time that I have met him.
